Lasagna

Description

This traditional Italian staple is the perfect blend of meat, pasta, cheese and sauce.

This recipe is easy for home cooks of all skill levels to follow. Just follow the step by step instructions and you will soon have a delicious lasagna ready for you.

Ingredients

  • Pasta Sheets
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Ground veal
  • Rocatta Cheese
  • Mozzarella
  • Parmesan

Steps

  1. Pre-Heat the Oven to 375 degrees farenheit
  2. Lay down a sheet of Pasta
  3. Spread Rocatta cheese on the pasta sheet
  4. Spread sauce over rocatta cheese, forming a thin but significant layer
  5. Spread Mozzarella Medallions uniformly on sauce
  6. Repeat steps 2-5 until you have formed 4 layers
  7. Top with parmesan cheese

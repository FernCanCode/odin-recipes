Steak

Description

This mouthwatering hunk of beef will encapsulate your tastebudes with beautifully carmelized flavors

Easy to prepare and a delight to enjoy. Try it out today.

Ingredients

  • Steak
  • Butter
  • Rosemary
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic Powder
  • Chili Powder

Steps

  1. Light your grill and bring it to temperature
  2. Season the steaks with salt, pepper, garlic powder and chili powder
  3. Sear on one side for 3 minutes or until crust has formed
  4. Flip and sear other side for same amount of time
  5. Move off of direct heat and into indirect heat until cooked to temperature
  6. Remove steaks at 135 degrees farenheit
  7. Garnish with Rosemary and Butter
  8. Allow to rest for 5 minutes
  9. Enjoy
